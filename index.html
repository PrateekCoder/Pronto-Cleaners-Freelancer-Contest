Pronto Cleaners: Paper-less, hassle-free, pocket-friendly. Your Personalized Laundndry Solution.

Pronto Cleaners believe that essential, everyday services should be provided in a fully personalized, painless way, to ensure that the customers have the time and peace of mind to focus on their primary life objectives and the pursuit of happiness. As the most innovative dry cleaners in Dubai, Pronto Cleaners offers a paper-less laundry solution. Our sales staff use handheld tablets to take your orders, and our invoices are texted and/or e-mailed to you.


Our digital approach and complete e-solution not only help us keep track of our customers' unique requirements and preferences, but also facilitate procedural improvements and service efficiency. The improved efficiency leads to a reduction in costs, which we pass on to our customers in the form of highly competitive and pocket-friendly prices.


We are partners with Linencraft - a division of Emirates Flight Catering Co. LLC. Linencraft is an ultra-modern, state-of-the-art industrial laundry facility. The meticulous attention to detail combined with a professionally-trained team ensure that service and quality standards are of exceptional and unrivaled excellence.
